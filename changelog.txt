v3.0
## Additions:
- New critters: Rams, Goats
- New blood moon enemies: Goat-head Fanatics, Cryptids
- New materials from Terrortoma and Cecitior 

## General Changes:
- Reorganized the mod's codebase (this doesn't affect the mod in any direct way but there's a risk for oversights)

## Changes to Items:
- Decreased the chance for giant fallen stars to drop special items
- Simplified the recipes for most hardmode evil biome weapons, removing the need for random pre-hm weapons
- Mawzooka, Fangnum, Vile Musket, and Equilibrium now need to be crafted at a demon/crimson altar 
- Nerfed the Gutling and moved it in progression
- Nerfed the Fang Slinger and made it a Screecher drop
- Adjusted the amount of materials used in certain recipes 

## Changes to Enemies:
- Reworked Hot Garbage
- Updated the Hookworm's coin drops, bestiary, and added a chance for it to drop a Whoopie Cushion
- Rotten Spine now only fires if the player is in its line of sight
- Moved the Bloodsucker to the Blood Moon
- Decreased the size of the Century Flower's spore hitbox
- Small Spectators are no longer targeted by minions
- Starlads now always drop one singular fallen star, and increased the chance for them to drop mushrooms
- Added a check for the giant fallen stars to not spawn Asteroid Herders if there are no players nearby
- Added death sounds for enemies that lacked them
- Nerfed most pre-hm enemy HP
- Nerfed most enemy spawn rates
- Removed the Dank Digger
- Removed the Rotjaw
- Removed the Vileglider
- Removed the Muckling
- Removed the Blood Hound
- Removed the Gnasher

## Fixes:
- Starlads, Asteriod Wardens, Asteriod Herders, and Belladonnas now scale their stats properly
- Fixed the Defibrillator's VFX being weirdly flat
- Fixed Cecitior's chomping attack rotating incorrectly
- Fixed the Cold Touch spawning incorrectly
- Fixed "Sounds/GenericExplosion" being missing
- Fixed certain lightning visuals
- The Hoarfrost Coiler no longer drops out of place loot
- Fixed the Tittering's Terrortoma-specific stats always being applied
- Fixed the Nasty Snapper glowing
- Fixed Rolypoly using incorrect gore sprites
