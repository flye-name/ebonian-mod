v3.0
## Additions:
- New critters: Rams, Goats
- New blood moon enemies: Goat-head Fanatics, Cryptids
- New materials from Terrortoma and Cecitior 

## General Changes:
- Reorganized the mod's codebase (this doesn't affect the mod in any direct way but there's a risk for oversights)
- Changed the way some special effects were rendered, fixing an issue where they would lag behind one frame
- Made the River of Starlight event rarer and not occur during blood moons or invasions
- Made the River of Starlight's unique fallen stars rarer
- The River of Starlight giant stars now don't always spawn herders, as intended
- Toned down the River of Starlight's visuals
- Made some music tracks quieter
- Nerfed the Jetpack Boulder movement
- The Jetpack Boulder now despawns if the player is too far away

## Changes to Items:
- Kodama Staff is now correctly obtainable
- Starbit is now correctly obtainable
- Decreased the chance for giant fallen stars to drop special items
- Increased the Cold Touch's mana usage
- Buffed the Warding Star, Botanist Staff
- The Shewstone now tries to bounce towards the cursor instead of just bouncing backwards
- Simplified the recipes for most hardmode evil biome weapons, removing the need for random pre-hm weapons
- Mawzooka, Fangnum, Vile Musket, and Equilibrium now need to be crafted at a demon/crimson altar 
- Nerfed the Gutling and moved it in progression
- Nerfed the Fang Slinger and made it a Screecher drop
- The Juvenile Brain no longer drops the Defibrillator, it instead drops the Bloodsoaked Dentures
- Removed the Bloodsoaked Dentures' recipe
- Changed the Defibrillator's recipe
- The Rusty Waraxe bleed effect now applies on critical hits instead of a set chance 
- Adjusted the amount of materials used in certain recipes 
- Seeping Brain and Rotten Heart no longer lower enemy spawns

## Changes to Bosses:
- Reworked Hot Garbage
- Xareus no longer despawns your summons when sheepened, instead, player projectiles no longer deal damage while sheepened
- Xareus no longer teleports players outside of the arena into it if they never participated in the fight to begin with 

## Changes to Enemies:
- Updated the Hookworm's coin drops, bestiary, and added a chance for it to drop a Whoopie Cushion
- Rotten Spine now only fires if the player is in its line of sight
- Updated the way Spectators spawn and made Massive Spectators and Cocoons rarer
- Massive Spectators and Cocoons now show up on the Lifeform Analyzer
- Moved the Bloodsucker to the Blood Moon
- Spectators now have a chance to drop lenses
- Jelleye Fish projectiles no longer go through tiles
- Decreased the size of the Century Flower's spore hitbox
- Small Spectators are no longer targeted by minions
- Starlads now always drop one singular fallen star, and increased the chance for them to drop mushrooms
- Added a check for the giant fallen stars to not spawn Asteroid Herders if there are no players nearby
- Added death sounds for enemies that lacked them
- Nerfed most pre-hm enemy HP
- Nerfed most enemy spawn rates
- Removed the Dank Digger
- Removed the Rotjaw
- Removed the Vileglider
- Removed the Muckling
- Removed the Blood Hound
- Removed the Gnasher

## Fixes:
- Starlads, Asteriod Wardens, Asteriod Herders, and Belladonnas now scale their stats properly
- Fixed the Defibrillator's VFX being weirdly flat
- Fixed Cecitior's chomping attack rotating incorrectly
- Fixed the Sheepening sheep not despawning once the player dies
- Fixed the Cold Touch spawning incorrectly
- Fixed Fleshformators still allowing you to use items when grabbing you
- Fixed "Sounds/GenericExplosion" being missing
- Fixed certain lightning visuals
- The Hoarfrost Coiler no longer drops out of place loot
- Fixed the Tittering's Terrortoma-specific stats always being applied
- Fixed the Nasty Snapper glowing
- Fixed Rolypoly using incorrect gore sprites
- Fixed the Cold Touch not climbing slopes
- Fixed the Naked Sheep item spawning normal sheep and being useable from any range
- Fix some incorrect tooltips
